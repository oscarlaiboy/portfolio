ADAMAS

Adamas is an app focused on Health Consciousness. The primary goal is to help college students involved in fitness achieve their primary fitness goals by monitoring their calorie intake, recommending healthy recipes, and a way to keep track of your foods!


My Role

  • UX/UI Design
  • Field Research
  • User Needs
  • Storyboarding
  • Wireframing
  • Moodboard
  • Design Language
  • Mockups

The Challenge

The topic of health conscious is really big and everyone has a different opinion and perspective on it. With so many different apps out in the world how can mine be different?

Research and User Needs

The first thing to do was to go out and do field observations in different areas relating to health conscious. I came up with a list of user needs based on my observations from the gym and grocery markets.


  • Needs a way to monitor own health

  • Wants to know what foods are healthy

  • Monitor his/her weight

  • Wants to look good and in shape
  • Consistent diet and routine

  • Need to create a fitness plan

  • Know how long food lasts

Speed Dating

I then created a few different storyboards to represent different ideas. I then did a few speed dating guides with the storyboards for feedback.



Information Architecture and Wireframes

Next I then created the information architecture and what main features the app would have. I then began to sketch and wireframe the screens.



Design Language System

I then created a moodboard by putting a bunch of pictures together based on adjectives that represent my idea. Based on the moodboard I then created a design language specification to show choice of colors, typefaces, and design elements.



Mockups

I then created mockups of our final screens for submission using Figma and then a click through prototype using Invision.

Presentation and Pitch

I then designed the layout for a poster presentation on this app. Putting together the whole process and pitching the idea.
