Redesign Study Abroad Website


This was a group project for my COGS187a class on information architecture and was also my first design project. The goal was to redesign the UCSD study abroad website. To do this we had to interview different people who would use the website and then redesign it so that it will be a lot better for users based on our interview data. We compared the UCSD study abroad website with multiple other study abroad websites from different colleges and universities to see what things are good for the website and what we liked about each one. After gathering all the info we then worked out some sketches on what things we wanted to put on each page.



The prototype that we had to use was Balsamiq to create some low fidelity functional wireframes. There were a lot of different utensils and options to play with. It was fun just trying out different things to just experiment and see what we like. It was a good way to get behind the design decisions made to design the pages.
