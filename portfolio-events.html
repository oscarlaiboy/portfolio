My Role

I was the lead product designer in charge of end to end design. Worked closely with product management, program manager, engineers, business analysts, data scientists, solution architects, and customer engagement team to meet deliveries. Managed roadmap and maintained on time deliveries.

The Challenge

This initiative is part of a multi year customer engagement project with a mining company. Customer co creation leveraging Maintenane Insights to provide insights into their business and help improve cost and production. The challenge was to help our customer in the mining industry track the different areas of operations and provide preventative maintenance with actionable insights to them so they can increase overall production output.
