IOT solution to help customers in the manufacturign industry digitze their work and bring great improvements in efficeincy.


My Role

I was the lead product designer in charge of the design alignment working closely with the product team. I was in charge of gathering requirements, defining roadmap and executing on deliverables.

The Challenge

Manufacturing Insights was created through collaboration across teams from India and Japan that didnâ€™t align to the Hitachi Vantara overall style guide and caused many gaps in the design and overall experience. Discovery

I worked with PM, PO, BA, and other designers through multiple sessions to get a full understanding of the application, any existing feedback from users, identifying inconsistencies, and how we got to the design.



UX Review

Next I did a full UX review of the application by diving into each screen in the build and trying to identify any usabilty issues, check components if there were any inconsistencies and which ones needed to align to the Hitachi style guide.


Iterate and Review
