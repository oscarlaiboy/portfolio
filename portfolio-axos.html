The Challenge

The challenge was to create a product selection tool for Axos Bank to recommend the best products for customers.

Research and User Flows

First I had to understand the different products of the bank in order to discover the different target audience. I also looked into articles about guided selling products and best practices on converting users through the process. Next, I created user flows to show the questions users will take under each path to reach a product.


Wireframing

For the design of the tool I started with paper sketches and then low-fidelity wireframes in Sketch. After many revisions and meetings I began to make high fidelity mockups.


Prototype and Usability Test

I used Invision to make an interactive prototype with my high fidelity screens. I then conducted a usability test for the prototype. The goals of the test was to see if the interface and experience was good and if users converted in the end. I used qualitative and quantitative metrics to measure my data.



Feedback and Looking Ahead

The feedback from the usability test was a success. The user experience was good and over 80% of participants converted in the end. There were also other design considerations to look over and I have updated changes to the product. In the future this guided selling tool will be developed and become a major tool for Axos Bank and their customers.
