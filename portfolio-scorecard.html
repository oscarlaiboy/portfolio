Performance Tracking

A feature in Lumada Maintenance Insights to operationalize operator insights and evaluate performance at a coal mine.


My Role

I was the lead product designer in charge of all design work related and working closely with product management, engineers, BA, data scientists, and customer engagement team to meet deliveries.

The Challenge

This initiative is part of a multi year customer engagement project with a mining company. Operators in the coal mine work on different assets all the time and they all contribute to the overall mine production. Supervisors of the operators need a high level view to validate how these operators are performing. They want to identify who is the top operator and why so they can find ways for other operators to increase production in the mine. This will also lead to lower costs on the assets they work on as well.

Discovery

The project team leads went on a customer onsite workshop session to discover our client needs and requirements. Got a chance to do onsite interviews, explore and shadow some workers, and do field research. We went through multiple workshop sessions to discover what our client really needed which led to the operator optimization piece.
